Welcom to AgroPi, the device to make gardening easy! We will help you monitor the environmental conditions of your plants, and notify you whenever the conditions are too far from the optimal ones, so that your plants never have to wither again.
When receiving your AgroPi, you can create an account, where you can edit your plants and view the current and previous conditions. Including the temperature, the humidity, the soil moisture and the light intensity.
